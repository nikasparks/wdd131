Overview

Purpose

This is site made promoting a group of photographers' work for purchase. It's an image gallery that displays the work of four different photographers the different styles that they focus on.

Audience

We are here to connect art seekers with artists. Our main demographic would be men and women 25-45 years old. People who are looking for ways to enrich their home or work space.

Dynamic elements

One main dynamic element is pulling the source reference in for from a JS object containing the information for the images on display.
